Company Overview

3PEAK is dedicated to the development of high-performance, high-quality, and highly reliable integrated circuit products. Our solutions include a broad range of signal chain analog chips, power management analog chips, and mixed-signal analog front ends. We serve a variety of industries including communication, industrial, automotive, new energy, and healthcare.

We went public on the STAR Market of the Shanghai Stock Exchange on September 21, 2020.
